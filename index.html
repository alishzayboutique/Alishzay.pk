Terms & Conditions

Effective: June 2026

1. How Ordering Works

We showcase our collections here as a digital lookbook. When you find an outfit you love, clicking the order button will connect you directly with our personal **WhatsApp Concierge channel**. Because we focus on premium fabrics, our inventory changes quickly. We will confirm if your selected fabric and size are available when you message us.

2. Pricing & Payments

All prices shown on our website are in **Pakistani Rupees (PKR)**. Shipping costs are calculated separately depending on your location, unless we are running a "Free Delivery" promotion in our top announcement bar. We will guide you through our secure payment options during our WhatsApp chat.

3. Custom & Atelier Orders

If you are ordering a custom-tailored piece through **The ALISHZAY Atelier**, we will double-check all your measurements with you before we begin. Once our tailors start cutting the fabric and assembling your bespoke outfit, we cannot accept any changes to the size, design, or measurements.

4. Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change! If you need to cancel an order, you must notify us via WhatsApp within **24 hours** of placing it. Once 24 hours have passed, the order goes into production and cannot be cancelled.

5. Return & Exchange Policy

Because our outfits are carefully crafted, **all sales are final**. Once a parcel is successfully delivered and paid for, it cannot be returned or exchanged under standard circumstances.

What to do if your item arrives damaged or defective:

If you receive a defective or damaged piece, please email us at alishzay.boutique@gmail.com within 2 days of delivery.

To help us verify the issue, your email must include:
1. A clear unboxing video (this video must be unedited/without cuts, showing the sealed package before it was opened, the full unboxing process, and the exact condition of the item).
2. Clear close-up photos of the defect.

Once our team verifies the damage, we will happily offer you a full refund or an exchange, based entirely on your preference. Your satisfaction is our utmost priority! 💕

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. We Don't Track You Secretly

Your digital privacy is safe with us. We do not use sneaky background tracking codes, cookies, or digital profiling scripts to monitor your movements across the internet. Anything you browse on our lookbook website stays entirely private within your own web browser.

2. Safe Shopping on WhatsApp & Social Media

Our website is an interactive digital catalog. When you click to buy a dress, join our broadcast circle, or ask a question, our site securely redirects you to official external platforms like WhatsApp, Instagram, or Facebook. Because these checkouts happen directly through those secure apps, your personal chat details are protected by their high-security networks.

3. How We Use Your Shipping Details

When you place an order with us on WhatsApp, we will ask for your name, phone number, delivery address, and sizing measurements. We only use this information to properly tailor your dress and safely ship your parcel to your doorstep. We never sell, rent, or share your contact details with outside marketing companies. Your information is strictly used to get your luxury outfits delivered smoothly.
